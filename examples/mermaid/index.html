Mermaid Diagrams

The theme applies Northwestern colors to Mermaid diagrams and adds a fullscreen viewer with pan/zoom, SVG download, source copying, and touch support. Hover any diagram below to see the toolbar.

See Getting Started for setup instructions.

    B -->|Yes| C[Great!]
    B -->|No| D[Debug]
    D --> B
    C --> E[Deploy]
